FEATURES

Unlimited, flexible savings targets

You can set anything as a goal in the PiggyPot app. Popular choices include holidays, birthdays, Christmas, a new phone and just for a rainy day. As a bonus you can add as many goals as you like and save for each individually.

Once you’ve started saving it’s simple to track the progress across all your savings pots at a glance, on the my pots screen.

Save up the way that suits you best

Pick how much you want to save, when by, pick any start date you like and if you want to make deposits on a weekly or monthly basis. You don’t have to calculate anything, we do that automatically and deposit money when you have specified.

Saving is automated with PiggyPot, meaning you can sit back and relax. You'll have saved up for the things you want in no time. Need to change when you save? No problem you can move your deposits forwards or backwards as required.

Get extra for free when you withdraw

That’s right! We give you a bonus on top of your savings when you withdraw as a digital gift card.

If you just want your savings returned to your bank account you can also do so for free. You can withdraw at anytime, even if you haven’t met your target.
